    Instructions

    - The soundboard is controlled by your keyboard numberpad. You can try pressing the corresponding numbers, but there are currently no sounds loaded on.
    - Open the sound panel on the left by clicking on one of the four options.
    - Once the panel is open, pick a sound and drag it onto one of the numbers.
    - Toggle the volume and speed of a sound by clicking the settings button (located on the bottom left of each box)
    - Delete the sound by clicking the delete icon (located on the bottom right of each box)
    - Stop all sound by pressing the space button
    - Clear all the contents of the soundboard by clicking the "X" located below the soundboard.
